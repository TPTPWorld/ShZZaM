There is a barbers' club that obeys the following three conditions:
(1) If any member A has shaved any other member B - whether himself or another - then all members 
have shaved A, though not necessarily at the same time.
(2) Four of the members are named Guido, Lorenzo, Petrucio, and Cesare.
(3) Guido has shaved Cesare.
Has Petrucio has shaved Lorenzo?
